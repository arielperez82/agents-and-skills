# GTM Plugin Catalog — Research & Outlines

**Date:** 2026-03-03
**Updated:** 2026-03-05 (expanded from 7 to 9 plugins based on expert panel review)
**Status:** Research complete, ready for build when prioritized

## Overview

9 self-contained plugins for Claude Cowork, composed from existing agents, skills, and commands in this repo. Each plugin will be a standalone ZIP (SKILL.md + references/ + scripts/) generated by a build script that cherry-picks and composes content from source skills.

**Packaging model:** `plugins/gtm/` contains only manifests and metadata. A build script reads the manifests, pulls content from source skills, composes self-contained SKILL.md files, and outputs versioned ZIP artifacts. No source duplication in the repo.

```
plugins/
  gtm/
    README.md                      # This catalog
    build.sh                       # Composes ZIPs from manifests
    manifests/
      content-seo.yaml             # Maps source skills -> plugin sections
      sales-prospecting.yaml
      sales-intelligence.yaml
      product-marketing.yaml
      demand-generation.yaml
      strategic-thinking.yaml
      gtm-doc-workshop.yaml
      gtm-strategy-icp.yaml
      revenue-marketing-ops.yaml
    dist/                          # Generated ZIPs (gitignored)
      content-seo-v1.0.0.zip
      sales-prospecting-v1.0.0.zip
      ...
```

## GTM Starter Packs (v2 enhancement)

Meta-bundles that recommend which plugins to install together. Not separate artifacts — these are catalog recommendations (README guidance or a "suggested packs" section in the plugin marketplace).

### Solo Founder GTM
**For:** Early-stage founders doing everything themselves.
**Plugins:** 1 (Content & SEO), 2 (Sales Prospecting), 4 (Product Marketing), 6 (Strategic Thinking), 8 (GTM Strategy & ICP)
**Why:** Covers the core loop: decide who to sell to (8), position the product (4), create content (1), prospect leads (2), think through hard decisions (6). Skips channel-specific and ops plugins until the team scales.

### Growth Team GTM
**For:** Startups with 3-10 person marketing/sales teams and specialized roles.
**Plugins:** All 9
**Why:** Full coverage. Each team member installs the 2-3 plugins for their role. The ops plugin (9) becomes the connective tissue.

### Enterprise Sales GTM
**For:** B2B companies with complex, high-ACV sales cycles.
**Plugins:** 2 (Sales Prospecting), 3 (Sales Intelligence), 4 (Product Marketing), 8 (GTM Strategy & ICP), 9 (Revenue & Mktg Ops)
**Why:** Emphasizes pipeline management, competitive positioning, account targeting, and revenue forecasting. Content and demand gen are secondary to deal execution.

### Content-Led Growth
**For:** Product-led or content-led companies where organic traffic drives pipeline.
**Plugins:** 1 (Content & SEO), 5 (Demand Gen & Channels), 7 (GTM Doc Workshop), 8 (GTM Strategy & ICP)
**Why:** Heavy on content production, SEO, email/LinkedIn distribution, and document creation. ICP plugin ensures content targets the right audience.

### ABM-First Sales
**For:** Companies running account-based programs as their primary GTM motion.
**Plugins:** 2 (Sales Prospecting), 3 (Sales Intelligence), 4 (Product Marketing), 8 (GTM Strategy & ICP), 9 (Revenue & Mktg Ops)
**Why:** ABM requires tight coordination between targeting (8), competitive positioning (4), personalized outreach (2), deal management (3), and attribution/measurement (9).

## Decision Log

### 2026-03-05: Expanded from 7 to 9 plugins

**Context:** Original catalog (2026-03-03) was built around 6 agents. By 2026-03-05, the repo had grown to 16 GTM agents and 27 GTM skills (17 marketing + 10 sales) through initiatives I27-GTMSI, I28-GTMMC, and I29-GTMSO.

**Decision:** Expert panel (5 members: SaaS GTM Leader, Dev Tools PM, MarOps Architect, Plugin Ecosystem Designer, RevOps VP) recommended expanding to 9 plugins with a 4-1 vote.

**Key arguments:**
- **Preserve original 7 mental models** — no reorganization for users who already know the catalog
- **Two genuinely new disciplines** — GTM Strategy (ICP/segmentation) and Revenue Operations are not subsets of existing plugins
- **Ops is one discipline** — marketing-ops, sales-ops, and revenue-ops bundled together because the same person asks all three questions
- **GTM Strategy separated from Product Marketing** — ICP/segmentation is quarterly; positioning/competitive is continuous. Different decision cadences = different plugins
- **9 is scannable** — passes the "read all names in 5 seconds" discoverability test

**Dissent (James, Plugin Ecosystem Designer):** Would ship 8, absorbing GTM Strategy into Product Marketing, then split if usage data justifies it. Principle: "you can split later but cannot easily merge."

**v2 enhancement agreed:** Starter packs as meta-bundles for persona-based discovery (Solo Founder, Growth Team, Enterprise Sales, Content-Led Growth, ABM-First Sales).
