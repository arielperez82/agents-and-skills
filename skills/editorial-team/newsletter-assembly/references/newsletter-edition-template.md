# Newsletter Edition Template

Use this template for every newsletter edition. All sections are required unless marked optional.

---

## Edition Structure

```markdown
# [Subject Line]

[Intro — 1-2 sentences max. Set context for today's edition.]

---

## [Story 1 Headline — Lead]

[Story 1 body — strongest news value, most broadly relevant]

---

## [Story 2 Headline — Middle]

[Story 2 body — deepest substance, most analytical]

---

## [Story 3 Headline — Close]

[Story 3 body — most engaging, shareable, emotionally resonant]

---

## Quick Poll

[Poll question — relates to at least one story above]

- [ ] Option A
- [ ] Option B
- [ ] Option C
- [ ] Option D

---

## Show Notes

**Topics covered:**
- [Topic 1 — one-line summary]
- [Topic 2 — one-line summary]
- [Topic 3 — one-line summary]

**Key quotes:**
- "[Quote 1]" — [Attribution]
- "[Quote 2]" — [Attribution]

**References:**
- [Source/report/person mentioned 1]
- [Source/report/person mentioned 2]

---

[Footer — publication name, unsubscribe, social links]
```

## Section Guidance

### Subject Line

- Under 60 characters
- Must relate to the lead story (no bait-and-switch)
- Preferred patterns: direct headline, teaser fact, question, number-driven, contrast

### Intro

- Maximum 2 sentences
- Establishes the edition's theme or simply says "here's what happened"
- Do not summarize all stories — let the reader discover them

### Story Ordering

| Position | Role | Selection Criteria |
|----------|------|-------------------|
| Lead (1) | Opens the edition | Strongest news value, broadest relevance |
| Middle (2) | Provides depth | Most analytical, most substance |
| Close (3) | Ends on a high | Most engaging, shareable, emotionally resonant |

### Poll

- 3-5 options (4 is the sweet spot)
- Relates to at least one story in this edition
- Options must be balanced (no obviously "correct" answer)
- Include one unexpected or provocative option

### Show Notes

- Topic list matches stories covered (may include additional topics not selected for full stories)
- Key quotes are verbatim with attribution
- References include all sources mentioned in the show

### Footer

- Publication name and tagline
- Unsubscribe link placeholder
- Social media links placeholder
