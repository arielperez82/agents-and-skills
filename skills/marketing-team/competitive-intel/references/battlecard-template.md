## Maintenance Guidelines

| Trigger | Action | Turnaround |
|---------|--------|-----------|
| Major product launch | Update sections 2, 3, 4, 5 | 48 hours |
| Pricing change | Update Overview + Win Strategy | 48 hours |
| Funding round | Update Overview | 1 week |
| Quarterly review | Full refresh of all sections | Scheduled |
| Win rate drops below threshold | Emergency review with sales team | 1 week |

## Effectiveness Tracking

Track these metrics to measure battlecard impact:
- **Competitive win rate** before and after battlecard deployment
- **Sales team usage** — how often is the battlecard accessed/shared?
- **Objection conversion rate** — do scripted responses work in practice?
- **Time to update** — are updates happening within the SLA above?
