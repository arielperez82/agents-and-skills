## Engagement Schedule

Daily engagement routine (30 minutes):

| Time | Action | Target |
|------|--------|--------|
| **Before posting** | Comment on 5-10 posts from target accounts | Industry leaders, prospects, peers |
| **Within 1 hour of posting** | Respond to all comments on your post | All commenters |
| **Throughout day** | Respond to new comments, DMs | Engagement maintenance |
| **Weekly** | Review analytics, adjust next week | Performance optimization |

## Content Repurposing Pipeline

Maximize each piece of content across formats:

```text
Blog Post (long-form)
  |
  +-- LinkedIn Article (republished, native formatting)
  +-- Carousel (key takeaways, 8-12 slides)
  +-- Text Post (one key insight, opinion angle)
  +-- Poll (ask audience about the topic)
  +-- Video (60s summary or commentary)
  +-- Twitter/X Thread (condensed version)
```

**Usage:** Copy this template monthly. Fill in topics during a 30-minute weekly planning session. Batch-create content 1-2 weeks ahead. Adjust based on monthly review performance data.
